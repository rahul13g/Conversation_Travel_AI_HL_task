# Destination: Europe (Paris + Switzerland / Rome + Santorini)

## Overview
Europe is our premium long-haul destination, combining iconic cities, breathtaking landscapes, and world-class culture. Most popular routes from India: Paris + Switzerland (Western Europe) or Rome + Santorini (Mediterranean). Schengen visa covers 26 countries — one trip, multiple countries possible.

## Best For
- Couples and honeymoon (Santorini, Paris)
- Families
- Friend groups
- Anniversary and milestone trips
- First-time long-haul travelers

## Travel Duration
- Standard: 8 Nights / 9 Days
- Extended: 10 Nights / 11 Days

## Visa
- Schengen Visa required for Indians
- Processing time: 15–20 working days (apply well in advance)
- Cost: ~₹8,000–10,000 (included in our packages)
- Covers 26 European countries
- Requires bank statement, travel insurance, hotel bookings as supporting documents

## Best Time to Visit
- Ideal: April–June (spring, pleasant weather, fewer crowds than peak)
- Peak: July–August (crowded, expensive but great weather)
- Autumn: September–October (beautiful foliage, manageable crowds)
- Avoid: December–February (cold, many attractions reduced hours)

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Schengen visa kitne time mein milta hai?
A: 15–20 working days. Hum full documentation support karte hain — bank statement format, insurance, itinerary sab. Ideally 6–8 weeks pehle apply karein.

Q: Paris aur Switzerland ek trip mein possible hai?
A: Haan, ek Schengen visa se possible hai. Paris se Switzerland train se 3.5 ghante ka safar hai — scenic route hai.

Q: Europe mein kaunsa season best hai Indians ke liye?
A: April–June best hai — weather pleasant, crowds manageable, aur prices peak se thoda kam hote hain.

Q: Kya honeymoon ke liye Europe recommend karoge?
A: Santorini Paris combination honeymoon ke liye ek dream trip hai. Premium package mein private sailboat, cliff hotel, candlelit dinners sab hote hain.

Q: Budget package mein kya kya cover hoga?
A: Flights, 3-star hotels, breakfast, airport transfers, visa. Sightseeing mostly self-guided hoga but major landmarks visit hote hain. Guided tours optional addon hain.

Q: Vegetarian options milenge Europe mein?
A: Haan, Paris, Rome aur Switzerland mein vegetarian options kaafi hain. Hum restaurants pre-identify karte hain trip ke hisaab se.
