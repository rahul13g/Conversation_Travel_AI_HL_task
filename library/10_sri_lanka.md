# Destination: Sri Lanka

## Overview
Sri Lanka is India's closest international destination — a teardrop-shaped island with extraordinary diversity: ancient ruins, lush tea estates, elephant sanctuaries, golden beaches, and whale watching, all within a compact geography. Exceptional value for money and a very short flight from India (1.5–2.5 hours from South India).

## Best For
- Families
- Couples
- Friend groups
- Solo travelers
- First-time international travelers
- Budget-conscious travelers wanting a rich experience

## Travel Duration
- Short trip: 5 Nights / 6 Days
- Standard: 7 Nights / 8 Days

## Visa
- ETA (Electronic Travel Authorization) required for Indians
- Apply online at eta.gov.lk — simple process
- Processing time: Instant to 24 hours
- Cost: ~₹1,200–1,500 (included in our packages)
- Valid for 30 days

## Best Time to Visit
- West and South Coast (Colombo, Galle, beaches): December–March
- East Coast (Trincomalee, Arugam Bay): May–September
- Hill Country (Kandy, Ella, Nuwara Eliya): Year-round, cooler climate
- Whale watching (Mirissa): November–April

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Sri Lanka kitna close hai India se?
A: Bahut close — Chennai se sirf 1.5 ghante ki flight hai. Delhi aur Mumbai se 3–3.5 ghante. Perfect for a quick international trip.

Q: Kya ye first international trip ke liye theek hai?
A: Ekdum perfect — English widely spoken hai, Indian food available hai, currency affordable hai (1 Indian Rupee ≈ 3–4 Sri Lankan Rupees), aur log bahut friendly hain.

Q: Safari Yala mein guaranteed leopard dikhega?
A: Guarantee nahi hoti wildlife mein, but Yala has one of the world's highest densities of leopards — chances bahut achhi hain, especially early morning safaris mein.

Q: Scenic train ride book karni padti hai advance mein?
A: Haan, Kandy to Ella first class observation car 30–45 days pehle book karni chahiye. Hum ye bhi handle karte hain.

Q: Budget mein Sri Lanka sabse value-for-money option hai?
A: ₹25,000–35,000 per person mein international trip — haan, Sri Lanka India ke paas sabse affordable aur richest-experience destination hai.
