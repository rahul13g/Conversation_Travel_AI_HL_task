# Destination: Dubai, UAE

## Overview
Dubai is India's most popular short-haul international destination. Famous for luxury experiences, desert adventures, world-class shopping, and iconic architecture. Short flight time (3–3.5 hours) makes it ideal for long weekends and quick getaways.

## Best For
- Couples and honeymoon
- Families with kids
- Friend groups
- Luxury travelers

## Travel Duration
- Short trip: 4 Nights / 5 Days
- Standard: 5 Nights / 6 Days

## Visa
- Visa required for Indians — included in all our packages
- Processing time: 3–5 working days
- Cost: ₹3,500–₹5,000 (included in package)
- Valid for 30 days, single entry

## Best Time to Visit
- Ideal: October to April
- Avoid: June–August (extreme heat, 40–45°C)
