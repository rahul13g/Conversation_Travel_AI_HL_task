# Destination: Singapore

## Overview
Singapore is a compact, ultra-clean city-state offering world-class attractions, food, shopping, and family entertainment. A top choice for families with children and first-time international travelers. Often combined with Malaysia or Bali for extended trips.

## Best For
- Families with children
- Couples
- Friend groups
- First-time international travelers

## Travel Duration
- Short trip: 4 Nights / 5 Days
- Standard: 5 Nights / 6 Days

## Visa
- Visa required for Indians — included in all our packages
- Processing time: 3–7 working days
- Valid for 30 days

## Best Time to Visit
- Year-round destination (tropical climate)
- February–April: least rainfall
- Avoid: November–January (heavier rains)
