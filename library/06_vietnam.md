# Destination: Vietnam (Hanoi + Ha Long Bay + Ho Chi Minh City / Hoi An)

## Overview
Vietnam is an emerging favorite among Indian travelers — offering stunning natural beauty, rich history, incredible street food, and exceptional value for money. Ha Long Bay is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Asia's most breathtaking experiences.

## Best For
- Friend groups
- Couples
- Solo travelers
- Adventure and culture seekers
- Budget-conscious travelers wanting premium experiences

## Travel Duration
- Standard: 6 Nights / 7 Days
- Extended: 8 Nights / 9 Days

## Visa
- E-Visa required for Indians
- Processing time: 3–5 working days online
- Cost: ~₹2,500–3,000 (included in our packages)
- Valid for 90 days, single or multiple entry

## Best Time to Visit
- North Vietnam (Hanoi, Ha Long): October–April
- South Vietnam (Ho Chi Minh, Hoi An): December–April
- Avoid: June–August (heavy rain in north)

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Ha Long Bay cruise kitni raat ka hota hai?
A: Budget mein 1 raat, Standard mein 2 raat, Luxury mein bhi 2 raat on a premium vessel. 2 raat recommended hai for full experience.

Q: Vietnam aur Bali mein se kya choose karein?
A: Vietnam culture, food, aur adventure seekers ke liye better hai. Bali beaches aur relaxation ke liye. Budget bhi similar range mein hai dono ka.

Q: Kya Hindi ya English mein kaam chal jaata hai?
A: Tourist areas mein English widely spoken hai. Hum local guide bhi arrange karte hain.

Q: Solo travel ke liye safe hai?
A: Haan, Vietnam solo travelers ke liye bahut safe hai aur ek vibrant backpacker community bhi hai.

Q: Visa khud apply karna padega?
A: Nahi, hum e-visa process aapke liye complete karte hain. Aapko sirf documents dene hote hain.
