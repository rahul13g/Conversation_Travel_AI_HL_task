# Destination: Bali, Indonesia

## Overview
Bali is our most popular destination for couples, friends, and solo travelers. Known for its lush rice terraces, temples, beaches, and vibrant nightlife. Best suited for those looking for a mix of relaxation, culture, and adventure.

## Best For
- Couples and honeymoon
- Friend groups
- Solo travelers

## Travel Duration
- Short trip: 5 Nights / 6 Days
- Standard: 6 Nights / 7 Days
- Extended: 7 Nights / 8 Days

## Visa
- Visa on Arrival — FREE for Indian passport holders
- No advance application needed
- Valid for 30 days

## Best Time to Visit
- Peak season: July–August, December
- Ideal season: April–June, September–October
- Monsoon (avoid): November–March

---

## Package Tiers

### Budget Package — ₹35,000 to ₹45,000 per person
- Flights: Round trip from major Indian cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore)
- Hotel: 3-star property, centrally located in Kuta or Seminyak
- Meals: Daily breakfast
- Transfers: Airport to hotel and back
- Duration: 5N/6D
- Activities: 1 group sightseeing tour included

### Standard Package — ₹55,000 to ₹70,000 per person
- Flights: Round trip included
- Hotel: 4-star property in Seminyak or Ubud
- Meals: Daily breakfast
- Transfers: Private airport transfers
- Duration: 6N/7D
- Activities: Ubud cultural tour + Tanah Lot sunset + 1 water sport activity

### Premium Package — ₹85,000 to ₹1,10,000 per person
- Flights: Round trip included
- Hotel: 5-star resort in Nusa Dua or Seminyak
- Meals: Breakfast + 2 special dinners
- Transfers: Private throughout
- Duration: 7N/8D
- Activities: Ubud tour, rice terrace trek, spa session, water sports, sunset dinner cruise

### Luxury Package — ₹1,40,000 and above per person
- Flights: Round trip (premium economy option available)
- Hotel: Private pool villa in Ubud or Seminyak
- Meals: All meals included + in-villa dining
- Transfers: Private car throughout stay
- Duration: 7N/8D or custom
- Activities: Fully curated — private driver, cooking class, Mount Batur sunrise trek, butler service

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Kya Bali mein visa chahiye?
A: Nahi, Indians ke liye Visa on Arrival hai jo bilkul free hai. Airport pe hi mil jaata hai.

Q: Akele travel karna safe hai Bali mein?
A: Haan, Bali solo travelers ke liye bahut safe hai, especially tourist areas mein.

Q: Budget mein best option kya hai?
A: 5N/6D Budget Package at ₹35,000–45,000 per person is our most popular entry point.

Q: Private pool villa kaunse package mein milta hai?
A: Luxury Package mein, starting from ₹1,40,000 per person.

Q: Activities alag se book karni padti hain?
A: Standard aur upar ke packages mein key activities included hain. Budget package mein additional activities optional hain.
