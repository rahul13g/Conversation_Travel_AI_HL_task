# Destination: Thailand (Bangkok + Phuket / Krabi)

## Overview
Thailand is our most affordable international destination and a top pick across all traveler types. Offers a perfect mix of city life, beaches, temples, and street food culture. Popular routes combine Bangkok with Phuket or Krabi.

## Best For
- Couples and honeymoon
- Friend groups
- Families
- Solo travelers
- First-time international travelers

## Travel Duration
- Short trip: 5 Nights / 6 Days
- Standard: 7 Nights / 8 Days

## Visa
- Visa on Arrival — FREE for Indian passport holders (as of 2024–25)
- No advance application needed
- Valid for 15–30 days

## Best Time to Visit
- Ideal: November to March
- Hot season: April–June (manageable)
- Monsoon (Phuket side wet): July–October

---

## Package Tiers

### Budget Package — ₹30,000 to ₹40,000 per person
- Flights: Round trip from major Indian cities
- Hotel: 3-star in Bangkok + 3-star beach hotel in Phuket or Krabi
- Meals: Daily breakfast
- Transfers: Airport transfers included
- Duration: 5N/6D (3N Bangkok + 2N Phuket)
- Activities: 1 group city tour included

### Standard Package — ₹50,000 to ₹65,000 per person
- Flights: Round trip included
- Hotel: 4-star in Bangkok + 4-star beachfront in Phuket or Krabi
- Meals: Daily breakfast
- Transfers: Private airport transfers
- Duration: 7N/8D (3N Bangkok + 4N Phuket/Krabi)
- Activities: Grand Palace tour, Phi Phi Islands day trip, island snorkeling

### Premium Package — ₹80,000 to ₹1,00,000 per person
- Flights: Round trip included
- Hotel: 5-star in Bangkok + 5-star beach resort
- Meals: Breakfast + 2 special Thai dinners
- Transfers: Private throughout
- Duration: 7N/8D
- Activities: Grand Palace, Phi Phi Islands, James Bond Island, elephant sanctuary visit, street food tour, spa

### Luxury Package — ₹1,30,000 and above per person
- Flights: Round trip (premium economy option)
- Hotel: Luxury 5-star or boutique resort
- Meals: All meals included
- Transfers: Private car throughout
- Duration: 7N/8D or custom
- Activities: Private yacht day, exclusive elephant sanctuary, Muay Thai show, fine dining, spa package

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Thailand ka sabse sasta package kya hai?
A: Budget package ₹30,000–40,000 per person mein available hai, 5N/6D ke liye.

Q: Family ke saath jaana theek hai?
A: Bilkul. Hum family-friendly itinerary banaate hain jisme cultural sites, beaches aur family activities sab included hote hain.

Q: Visa ke liye kuch karna padega?
A: Nahi, Indians ko Thailand mein Visa on Arrival milta hai, free mein. Airport pe hi process ho jaata hai.

Q: Bangkok aur Phuket dono cover ho sakte hain?
A: Haan, hamare standard aur premium packages mein dono destinations cover hote hain.

Q: Solo travel safe hai?
A: Haan, Thailand solo travelers ke liye ek safest aur most backpacker-friendly destinations mein se ek hai.
