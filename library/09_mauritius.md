# Destination: Mauritius

## Overview
Mauritius is a stunning Indian Ocean island nation known for its white-sand beaches, turquoise lagoons, luxury resorts, and the famous underwater waterfall optical illusion. A top honeymoon and romantic destination for Indian couples, offering a French-Creole cultural blend with excellent accessibility from India.

## Best For
- Honeymoon couples (top choice)
- Anniversary trips
- Families
- Couples seeking beach + luxury combination

## Travel Duration
- Short trip: 5 Nights / 6 Days
- Standard: 6 Nights / 7 Days

## Visa
- Visa on Arrival — FREE for Indian passport holders
- No advance application needed
- Valid for 60 days

## Best Time to Visit
- Ideal: May–December (dry season, cooler)
- Peak: July–August (school holidays, book early)
- Avoid: January–March (cyclone season, heavy rain risk)

## Package Tiers

### Budget Package — ₹70,000 to ₹90,000 per person
- Flights: Round trip from major Indian cities
- Hotel: 3-star hotel or guesthouse, non-beachfront
- Meals: Daily breakfast
- Transfers: Airport transfers included
- Duration: 5N/6D
- Activities: North Island tour, Chamarel 7 Coloured Earth visit

### Standard Package — ₹1,10,000 to ₹1,40,000 per person
- Flights: Round trip included
- Hotel: 4-star beachfront resort
- Meals: Half board (breakfast + dinner)
- Transfers: Private airport transfers
- Duration: 6N/7D
- Activities: Catamaran cruise with snorkeling + BBQ lunch, island tour, Chamarel, waterfall visit, water sports (kayak, paddleboard)

### Premium Package — ₹1,80,000 to ₹2,30,000 per person
- Flights: Round trip included
- Hotel: 5-star luxury beachfront resort
- Meals: All inclusive (all meals + selected beverages)
- Transfers: Private throughout
- Duration: 6N/7D
- Activities: Full-day catamaran cruise, underwater waterfall aerial view tour, parasailing, sunset cruise, dolphin watching, spa session, Ile aux Cerfs island day trip

### Luxury Package — ₹3,00,000 and above per person
- Flights: Round trip (premium economy or business class option)
- Hotel: Private beach villa or ultra-luxury resort (One&Only, Four Seasons, Constance)
- Meals: All inclusive — gourmet dining, fine wines
- Transfers: Chauffeur-driven throughout
- Duration: 6N/7D or custom
- Activities: Private catamaran charter, helicopter tour over underwater waterfall, butler service, in-villa couple's spa, private beach dinner, exclusive excursions

## Top Activities
1. Catamaran cruise — snorkeling, BBQ on board, swimming with dolphins
2. Underwater waterfall — helicopter or aerial view of optical illusion off Le Morne
3. Ile aux Cerfs — pristine private island with water sports
4. Chamarel — 7 Coloured Earth, waterfall, rum distillery
5. Dolphin watching — swim with wild dolphins at Tamarin Bay
6. Parasailing over turquoise lagoon
7. Glass-bottom boat tour
8. Black River Gorges National Park — hiking, wildlife
9. Port Louis market and Caudan Waterfront
10. Sunset beach walk and couple's spa

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Mauritius honeymoon ke liye kitna best hai?
A: Ek dum perfect hai — white beaches, luxury resorts, romantic cruises, aur peaceful atmosphere. Hum special honeymoon amenities bhi arrange karte hain resorts se.

Q: Kya Mauritius mein visa chahiye?
A: Nahi, Indians ko Visa on Arrival milta hai, bilkul free. Airport pe stamp ho jaata hai.

Q: Underwater waterfall kaise dekh sakte hain?
A: Helicopter tour se aerial view milta hai — ye ek optical illusion hai jo sand and silt se banta hai. Premium aur Luxury packages mein included hai.

Q: All inclusive ka kya matlab hai?
A: Teen waqt ka khana, snacks, selected beverages (alcoholic bhi) — sab resort mein included. Bahar jaane ki zaroorat nahi.

Q: Maldives vs Mauritius — kaunsa choose karein?
A: Maldives zyada secluded aur water-centric hai. Mauritius mein culture, activities, aur island exploration bhi hai. Mauritius families ke liye bhi better suited hai.
