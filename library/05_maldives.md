# Destination: Maldives

## Overview
Maldives is the ultimate romantic and luxury destination — known for its crystal-clear turquoise waters, overwater bungalows, and pristine coral reefs. A dream destination for honeymoon couples and luxury seekers. Also increasingly popular for anniversary trips and babymoons.

## Best For
- Honeymoon couples (most popular)
- Anniversary trips
- Couples looking for luxury and seclusion
- Water sports and diving enthusiasts

## Travel Duration
- Short trip: 4 Nights / 5 Days
- Standard: 5 Nights / 6 Days

## Visa
- Visa on Arrival — FREE for Indian passport holders
- No advance application needed
- Valid for 30 days

## Best Time to Visit
- Ideal: November to April (dry season)
- Shoulder: May–June (some rain, fewer crowds, better rates)
- Avoid: July–October (heavy monsoon season)

## Package Tiers

### Budget Package — ₹60,000 to ₹80,000 per person
- Flights: Round trip to Malé + speedboat transfer to island
- Hotel: Guesthouse on a local island (e.g., Maafushi or Dhigurah)
- Meals: Daily breakfast
- Transfers: Speedboat from Malé airport
- Duration: 4N/5D
- Activities: Snorkeling trip, dolphin watching cruise, beach access

### Standard Package — ₹1,00,000 to ₹1,30,000 per person
- Flights: Round trip to Malé + speedboat transfer
- Hotel: 4-star resort on a private island
- Meals: Half board (breakfast + dinner)
- Transfers: Speedboat transfers included
- Duration: 5N/6D
- Activities: Snorkeling, water sports (kayak, paddleboard), sunset cruise, sandbank visit

### Premium Package — ₹1,60,000 to ₹2,20,000 per person
- Flights: Round trip to Malé + seaplane or speedboat transfer
- Hotel: 5-star resort, beach villa or water bungalow
- Meals: Full board (all meals)
- Transfers: Seaplane or private speedboat
- Duration: 5N/6D
- Activities: Scuba diving (2 dives), sunset fishing, sandbank private picnic, couples spa, dolphin cruise, all water sports

### Luxury Package — ₹3,00,000 and above per person
- Flights: Round trip (premium economy option available)
- Hotel: Overwater bungalow at a premium resort (Conrad, One&Only, Soneva, Four Seasons)
- Meals: All inclusive — all meals, select beverages
- Transfers: Seaplane private transfer
- Duration: 5N/6D or custom
- Activities: Private butler, underwater restaurant dinner, private yacht, scuba diving, in-villa spa, glass floor villa experience

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Maldives mein honeymoon ke liye kaunsa package best hai?
A: Premium ya Luxury package best hai honeymoon ke liye — overwater bungalow, private dinners, spa sab mila ke ek unforgettable experience hota hai.

Q: Budget mein Maldives possible hai?
A: Haan, local island guesthouses pe ₹60,000–80,000 per person mein jaana possible hai. Beaches aur snorkeling free hote hain.

Q: Seaplane aur speedboat mein kya farak hai?
A: Seaplane zyada remote aur exclusive resorts tak jaata hai — stunning aerial views bhi milte hain. Speedboat nearby islands ke liye theek hai. Premium+ packages mein seaplane included hai.

Q: Maldives mein alcohol milta hai?
A: Resort islands pe milta hai, local islands pe nahi. Sab package resort islands pe hain toh available hoga.

Q: Sabse best time kab jaayein?
A: November se April — dry season, clear water, perfect snorkeling conditions.
