# Destination: Uttarakhand (Rishikesh + Mussoorie + Jim Corbett + Auli + Char Dham)

## Overview
Uttarakhand is India's spiritual and adventure heartland — from the yoga capital of the world in Rishikesh to snow-covered Auli ski slopes, from Jim Corbett's tigers to the sacred Char Dham pilgrimage circuit. One of the most accessible mountain destinations from Delhi, making it ideal for long weekends and short breaks.

## Best For
- Adventure seekers (rafting, trekking, skiing)
- Spiritual and pilgrimage travelers
- Wildlife enthusiasts
- Families
- Couples (Mussoorie)
- Delhi-NCR travelers for quick mountain escapes

## Travel Duration
- Weekend: 2 Nights / 3 Days (Rishikesh or Mussoorie)
- Standard: 5 Nights / 6 Days
- Extended: 8 Nights / 9 Days (Char Dham)

## Getting There
- Flights to Dehradun (Jolly Grant Airport) — connected from Delhi, Mumbai
- Train to Haridwar or Dehradun from Delhi (4–5 hours)
- Road: Delhi to Rishikesh ~5 hours, Delhi to Mussoorie ~6 hours

## Best Time to Visit
- Rishikesh/Mussoorie: Year-round. Best March–June, September–November
- Auli (Skiing): December–March
- Jim Corbett: November–June (park open, best wildlife sighting)
- Char Dham: May–June, September–October (temples open)

- Transport: Train + local transport
- Hotel: 3-star
- Meals: Daily breakfast
- Duration: 3N/4D (Rishikesh + Haridwar)

- Transport: Flights or train included
- Hotel: 4-star resort
- Meals: Breakfast + 1 dinner
- Transfers: Private cab
- Duration: 5N/6D
- Activities: White water rafting (16km stretch), bungee jump, camping by Ganga, Mussoorie, Kempty Falls, yoga session, Haridwar Ganga Aarti

- Transport: Flights included
- Hotel: Luxury riverside camp or 5-star resort

### Luxury Package — ₹80,000 and above per person
- Transport: Flights included

## Zone Highlights

### Rishikesh — Adventure + Spirituality
- White water rafting on the Ganga — Grade 3–4 rapids (16km, 26km stretches)
- Bungee jumping — India's highest at 83m (Jumpin Heights)
- Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat and Parmarth Niketan
- Beatles Ashram (Chaurasi Kutia) — where Beatles meditated in 1968
- Yoga and meditation ashrams
- Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula suspension bridges
- Camping on Ganga banks

### Mussoorie — Queen of Hills
- Mall Road — British era promenade
- Kempty Falls
- Gun Hill — cable car ride, Himalayan views
- Camel's Back Road — sunset walks
- Landour — charming old cantonment area, Ruskin Bond's home town

### Jim Corbett National Park
- India's oldest national park
- Best for Royal Bengal Tiger sightings
- Elephant safari and jeep safari
- Dhikala zone — best for wildlife, requires advance booking
- Corbett Museum

### Auli — Ski Resort
- India's best skiing destination
- Gondola (cable car) from Joshimath — one of Asia's longest
- Nanda Devi and Kamet peaks views
- Skiing, snowboarding, snow activities
- Base for Valley of Flowers trek (summer)

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Rafting ke liye experience chahiye?
A: Bilkul nahi — 16km stretch beginners ke liye perfect hai. Guide saath hota hai. Minimum age 14 years. Swimming nahi aata toh bhi life jacket ke saath safe hai.

Q: Bungee jumping safe hai?
A: Jumpin Heights India ka most certified adventure company hai — international safety standards follow karte hain. Thousands log har season jump karte hain.

Q: Jim Corbett mein tiger guarantee milega?
A: Guarantee nahi hoti wildlife mein, but Corbett mein chances bahut achhe hain — especially Dhikala zone mein. Hum advance zone permit arrange karte hain.

Q: Char Dham Yatra ke liye kitne din chahiye?
A: Complete Char Dham (Yamunotri + Gangotri + Kedarnath + Badrinath) ke liye minimum 10–12 days. Helicopter packages 5–6 days mein complete ho jaate hain.

Q: Delhi se Rishikesh kitne time mein?
A: Road se ~5 hours (Haridwar ~4 hours). Train se Haridwar Shatabdi 4.5 hours. Dehradun flight 45 minutes, phir road se 1.5 hours.
