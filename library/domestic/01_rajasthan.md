# Destination: Rajasthan (Jaipur + Udaipur + Jodhpur + Jaisalmer)

## Overview
Rajasthan is India's most iconic cultural destination — a land of grand palaces, desert landscapes, vibrant bazaars, and royal heritage. Popular for couples, families, and international-style experiences within India. Covers multiple cities each with a distinct character.

## Best For
- Couples and honeymoon
- Families
- Cultural and heritage lovers
- Photography enthusiasts
- First-time domestic travelers wanting a rich experience

## Travel Duration
- Short: 4 Nights / 5 Days (2 cities)
- Standard: 6 Nights / 7 Days (3 cities)
- Extended: 8 Nights / 9 Days (4 cities)

## Getting There
- Flights to Jaipur, Udaipur, or Jodhpur from all major Indian cities
- Train: Well-connected via Rajdhani and Shatabdi from Delhi and Mumbai
- Road: Driveable from Delhi (Jaipur ~5 hours)

## Best Time to Visit
- Ideal: October to March (cool, pleasant)
- Peak: November–February (festivals, Desert Festival in Jaisalmer)
- Avoid: April–June (extreme heat, 40–48°C)

## Package Tiers

### Budget Package — ₹15,000 to ₹20,000 per person
- Transport: Train or budget flight
- Hotel: 3-star throughout
- Meals: Daily breakfast
- Transfers: Shared or local transport
- Duration: 4N/5D (Jaipur + Udaipur)
- Activities: City palace visits, local markets, auto rickshaw tours

### Standard Package — ₹25,000 to ₹35,000 per person
- Transport: Flights included or train + intercity cab
- Hotel: 4-star heritage property
- Meals: Breakfast + 1 traditional Rajasthani dinner
- Transfers: Private cab throughout
- Duration: 6N/7D (Jaipur + Jodhpur + Udaipur)
- Activities: City Palace, Mehrangarh Fort, Lake Pichola boat ride, local bazaar tour, cultural show

### Premium Package — ₹50,000 to ₹70,000 per person
- Transport: Flights included
- Hotel: Heritage palace hotels (Neemrana, Taj, Oberoi class)
- Meals: Breakfast + 2 special dinners (one lakeside, one rooftop)
- Transfers: Private luxury cab throughout
- Duration: 7N/8D (all 4 cities)
- Activities: Desert safari in Jaisalmer, camel ride, hot air balloon over Jaipur, private palace tour, royal Rajasthani thali dinner

### Luxury Package — ₹1,00,000 and above per person
- Transport: Flights (premium economy option)
- Hotel: Luxury palace hotels — Umaid Bhawan, Taj Lake Palace, RAAS
- Meals: All meals included — curated royal dining
- Transfers: Private vintage car or luxury SUV throughout
- Duration: 8N/9D or custom
- Activities: Private heritage walks, Luxury train option (Palace on Wheels), exclusive access experiences, hot air balloon, private desert camp

## City Highlights

### Jaipur — The Pink City
- Amber Fort — hilltop fort with elephant rides
- City Palace and Jantar Mantar
- Hawa Mahal — iconic pink facade
- Johari Bazaar — gems, jewelry, textiles
- Hot air balloon at sunrise

### Udaipur — The City of Lakes
- Lake Pichola — boat ride to Jag Mandir
- City Palace — largest palace complex in Rajasthan
- Jagdish Temple
- Saheliyon ki Bari gardens
- Rooftop dining with lake views

### Jodhpur — The Blue City
- Mehrangarh Fort — one of India's largest forts
- Jaswant Thada — marble cenotaph
- Clock Tower market — spices, handicrafts
- Blue rooftop views of the old city

### Jaisalmer — The Golden City
- Jaisalmer Fort — living fort with havelis inside
- Sam Sand Dunes — desert safari, camel ride, cultural show
- Patwon Ki Haveli
- Gadisar Lake at sunrise

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Rajasthan mein sabse best city kaunsi hai?
A: Har city alag experience deti hai — Jaipur grandeur ke liye, Udaipur romance ke liye, Jodhpur history ke liye, Jaisalmer desert adventure ke liye. Ideal hai sab cover karna.

Q: Honeymoon ke liye Rajasthan theek hai?
A: Udaipur India ka sabse romantic city hai — lakeside palaces, candlelit dinners, boat rides. Honeymoon ke liye perfect.

Q: Desert safari mein kya hota hai?
A: Camel ride, jeep safari on sand dunes, sunset views, local cultural performance (folk music and dance), aur overnight camp option.

Q: Kya train se jaana better hai ya flight?
A: Short trip ke liye flight better — time bachta hai. Longer circuit ke liye train + private cab combo best hai.

Q: Budget mein best option kya hai Rajasthan mein?
A: Jaipur + Udaipur 4N/5D package ₹15,000–20,000 per person mein excellent value deta hai.
