# Destination: Ladakh (Leh + Nubra Valley + Pangong Lake + Zanskar)

## Overview
Ladakh is India's most dramatic and otherworldly destination — a high-altitude cold desert with turquoise lakes, ancient monasteries, stark mountain landscapes, and some of the world's most iconic roads. A bucket-list destination for adventurers, bikers, and anyone seeking something truly extraordinary.

## Best For
- Adventure seekers and bikers
- Trekkers
- Photography enthusiasts
- Solo travelers
- Couples seeking unique experiences
- Nature and spirituality seekers

## Travel Duration
- Short: 5 Nights / 6 Days (Leh + Pangong)
- Standard: 7 Nights / 8 Days
- Extended: 10 Nights / 11 Days (including Zanskar)

## Getting There
- Flights: Direct to Leh from Delhi, Mumbai, Srinagar, Jammu (book early — limited seats)
- Road: Manali–Leh Highway (open May–October, ~2 days) or Srinagar–Leh Highway (open May–November)
- Bike trip: Popular route — Delhi to Leh via Manali (~7–10 days)

## Acclimatization
IMPORTANT: First day in Leh must be complete rest. Altitude is 3,500m — rushing activities causes AMS (Altitude Mountain Sickness). All our packages include acclimatization day.

## Best Time to Visit
- Peak: June–September (all roads open, all lakes accessible)
- Shoulder: May, October (fewer tourists, some roads closed)
- Winter (November–March): Frozen Zanskar River — Chadar Trek only

## Top Experiences

### Pangong Tso Lake
- 134km long lake, 60% in China
- Famous from 3 Idiots film
- Color changes from blue to green to red through the day
- Overnight camping on the banks — star-filled sky
- Best time: June–September

### Nubra Valley
- Cross Khardung La — world's highest motorable road at 5,359m
- Hunder Sand Dunes — unique cold desert within mountains
- Double-hump Bactrian camel rides
- Diskit Monastery with giant Maitreya Buddha statue
- Turtuk village — last Indian village near Pakistan border

### Monasteries
- Hemis — largest and wealthiest monastery in Ladakh
- Thiksey — resembles Potala Palace in Lhasa
- Diskit — dramatic hilltop location in Nubra
- Lamayuru — 'moonland' landscape surrounding

### Adventure
- Rafting on Zanskar and Indus Rivers
- Trekking — Markha Valley, Stok Kangri, Chadar (winter)
- Mountain biking
- Paragliding near Leh
- Rock climbing

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Altitude sickness se kaise bachein?
A: Pehle din complete rest karo Leh mein. Paani zyada piyo. Diamox (medicine) doctor se le sakte ho. Pehle 48 hours mein koi physical activity mat karo.

Q: Bike trip kitne din mein hoti hai Leh tak?
A: Delhi to Leh via Manali approximately 7–10 days depending on stops. Enfield ya Royal Enfield Himalayan recommended. We can arrange guided bike trips.

Q: Pangong pe tent mein rehna safe hai?
A: Haan, our premium camps are comfortable with proper bedding, attached washrooms, and heating. Temperature raat mein -5°C tak gir sakta hai even in summer.

Q: Kya pura Ladakh ek trip mein cover ho sakta hai?
A: 10–12 days mein Leh, Nubra, Pangong, Tso Moriri, aur Zanskar cover ho sakta hai. 7 days mein Leh, Nubra, aur Pangong comfortably cover hote hain.

Q: Inner Line Permit kya hota hai?
A: Nubra, Pangong, Tso Moriri ke liye Indian nationals ko ILP chahiye. Hum permit process handle karte hain — aapko kuch nahi karna.
