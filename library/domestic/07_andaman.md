# Destination: Andaman & Nicobar Islands (Port Blair + Havelock + Neil Island)

## Overview
The Andaman Islands offer India's finest beach and underwater experience — crystal clear turquoise water, pristine white sand beaches, world-class scuba diving and snorkeling, and a laid-back island vibe. Often compared to Southeast Asian islands like Bali and Phuket, but without the need for a passport.

## Best For
- Couples and honeymoon
- Beach lovers
- Scuba diving and snorkeling enthusiasts
- Friends groups
- Solo travelers

## Travel Duration
- Short: 4 Nights / 5 Days
- Standard: 6 Nights / 7 Days
- Extended: 8 Nights / 9 Days

## Getting There
- Flights: Direct to Port Blair from Chennai, Kolkata, Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad
- Ferry: From Chennai (56 hours by ship — not recommended for most travelers)
- Inter-island: Government ferries or private speedboats between islands

## Best Time to Visit
- Ideal: October to May
- Peak: December to February
- Avoid: June–September (monsoon, rough seas, most water activities suspended)

## Package Tiers

### Budget Package — ₹15,000 to ₹22,000 per person
- Transport: Budget flights to Port Blair
- Hotel: 3-star in Port Blair + basic beach resort in Havelock
- Meals: Daily breakfast
- Transfers: Government ferry between islands
- Duration: 4N/5D (Port Blair + Havelock)
- Activities: Radhanagar Beach, basic snorkeling, Cellular Jail sound and light show, Ross Island

### Standard Package — ₹30,000 to ₹42,000 per person
- Transport: Flights included
- Hotel: 4-star throughout
- Meals: Breakfast + 1 beachside seafood dinner
- Transfers: Private speedboat between islands + private cab
- Duration: 6N/7D (Port Blair + Havelock + Neil)
- Activities: Scuba diving (2 dives), glass-bottom boat, Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach snorkeling, Cellular Jail, Ross Island, Laxmanpur Beach Neil

### Premium Package — ₹60,000 to ₹80,000 per person
- Transport: Flights included
- Hotel: 5-star beach resort (Taj Exotica Havelock or Barefoot at Havelock)
- Meals: Breakfast + 2 special dinners
- Transfers: Private speedboat + private transfers
- Duration: 7N/8D
- Activities: PADI scuba certification or advanced dives, sea walk, kayaking through mangroves, night kayaking (bioluminescence), sunset cruise, private beach picnic, Barren Island volcano trip (if available)

### Luxury Package — ₹1,00,000 and above per person
- Transport: Flights included
- Hotel: Taj Exotica or Symphony Palms exclusive
- Meals: All inclusive
- Transfers: Private throughout
- Duration: 7N/8D or custom
- Activities: Private dive guide, charter speedboat, exclusive beach experiences, helicopter trip option

## Island Highlights

### Port Blair — Gateway
- Cellular Jail — colonial prison, sound and light show (must watch)
- Ross Island — abandoned British colonial ruins, deer roaming free
- Corbyn's Cove Beach
- Anthropological Museum — Andaman tribes
- Chatham Saw Mill

### Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep) — The Gem
- Radhanagar Beach (Beach 7) — one of Asia's best beaches (Time Magazine)
- Elephant Beach — best snorkeling spot
- Kalapathar Beach — rocky, serene, sunrise
- Scuba diving — the main draw, visibility up to 30m
- Night kayaking — bioluminescent plankton
- Mangrove kayaking

### Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep) — The Quiet One
- Laxmanpur Beach — stunning natural rock bridge
- Bharatpur Beach — calm lagoon, water sports
- Sitapur Beach — best sunrise point
- Quiet, uncrowded, rustic — ideal for couples wanting peace

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Kya scuba diving ke liye experience chahiye?
A: Nahi — beginner dives (Discover Scuba) mein koi experience nahi chahiye. Instructor ke saath first dive 30 minutes mein ho jaati hai. PADI certification bhi humse le sakte ho.

Q: Radhanagar Beach wakai Asia ki best beach hai?
A: Time Magazine ne 2004 mein is award diya tha — aur aaj bhi ye India ki most stunning beach mani jaati hai. Crystal clear water, white sand, surrounded by forest.

Q: Islands ke beech ferry ya speedboat?
A: Government ferry affordable hai (₹400-600 one way) but crowded aur 2 hours lagti hai. Private speedboat premium option hai — 45-60 minutes. Standard aur upar ke packages mein private speedboat included hai.

Q: Bioluminescent kayaking kya hota hai?
A: Raat mein mangrove creeks mein kayaking — paani mein tiny plankton hote hain jo disturb hone pe blue glow karte hain. Magical experience. Premium packages mein included.

Q: Monsoon mein Andaman jaana theek hai?
A: June–September mein most water activities band hote hain rough seas ki wajah se. October start se best time hai.
