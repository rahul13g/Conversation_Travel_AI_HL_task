# Destination: Himachal Pradesh (Manali + Shimla + Kasol + Spiti)

## Overview
Himachal Pradesh is India's premier mountain destination — snow-capped peaks, alpine valleys, adventure sports, and charming hill stations. Manali and Shimla are family and couple favourites, while Kasol and Spiti attract trekkers, backpackers, and adventure seekers. A year-round destination with different experiences each season.

## Best For
- Adventure seekers and trekkers
- Couples and honeymoon (Manali, Shimla)
- Friends groups
- Solo travelers and backpackers (Kasol, Spiti)
- Families (Shimla, Manali)

## Travel Duration
- Short: 4 Nights / 5 Days (Shimla + Manali)
- Standard: 6 Nights / 7 Days
- Extended: 9 Nights / 10 Days (including Spiti)

## Getting There
- Flights to Chandigarh or Bhuntar (Kullu-Manali Airport)
- Train to Kalka, then toy train to Shimla (heritage experience)
- Road: Delhi to Shimla ~7 hours, Delhi to Manali ~14 hours (or overnight bus)

## Best Time to Visit
- Shimla: Year-round. Snow: December–February
- Manali: May–June (green), October–November (snow begins), December–February (heavy snow, Rohtang closed)
- Kasol: April–June, September–November
- Spiti: May–October only (road closed in winter)

## Package Tiers

### Budget Package — ₹10,000 to ₹15,000 per person
- Transport: Volvo bus or budget flight to Chandigarh + local transport
- Hotel: 3-star or guesthouse
- Meals: Daily breakfast
- Transfers: Local cabs or shared jeeps
- Duration: 4N/5D (Shimla + Manali)
- Activities: Mall Road, Rohtang Pass (if open), Solang Valley, local sightseeing

### Standard Package — ₹20,000 to ₹30,000 per person
- Transport: Flights to Chandigarh included
- Hotel: 4-star throughout
- Meals: Breakfast + 1 bonfire dinner
- Transfers: Private cab throughout
- Duration: 6N/7D
- Activities: Rohtang Pass, Solang Valley snow activities, Hadimba Temple, Old Manali, Shimla heritage walk, Kufri

### Premium Package — ₹45,000 to ₹60,000 per person
- Transport: Flights included
- Hotel: Mountain resort with valley views — Span Resort, Honeymoon Inn class
- Meals: Breakfast + 2 special dinners
- Transfers: Private luxury cab throughout
- Duration: 7N/8D
- Activities: River rafting on Beas, paragliding in Solang, snow activities, Kasol/Kheerganga trek day, Naggar Castle visit, apple orchard visit, bonfire with barbeque

### Luxury Package — ₹80,000 and above per person
- Transport: Flights included
- Hotel: Luxury mountain lodges or boutique properties
- Meals: All meals included
- Transfers: Private throughout
- Duration: 8N/9D or custom
- Activities: Helicopter ride over Rohtang, private trekking guide, premium adventure package, exclusive valley camps

## Zone Highlights

### Shimla — The Queen of Hills
- Mall Road and Ridge — colonial-era promenade
- Jakhu Temple — monkeys and mountain views
- Christ Church — British heritage
- Kufri — short snow excursion, mini zoo
- Toy Train — Kalka to Shimla UNESCO heritage railway

### Manali — Adventure Capital
- Rohtang Pass — snow, views (permit required)
- Solang Valley — skiing, snow activities, paragliding
- Hadimba Devi Temple — ancient wooden temple in deodar forest
- Old Manali — cafes, hippie vibe, backpacker scene
- River rafting on Beas River
- Naggar Castle

### Kasol — Backpacker Heaven
- Parvati Valley trekking base
- Kheerganga Trek — hot springs at top
- Chalal Village walk
- Manikaran Gurudwara — natural hot springs
- Tosh and Kalgha villages

### Spiti Valley — The Cold Desert
- Key Monastery — dramatic hilltop monastery
- Chandratal Lake — stunning crater lake
- Kaza town
- Pin Valley National Park — Snow Leopard territory
- Dhankar Monastery and lake

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Manali aur Shimla mein se kaunsa better hai?
A: Different vibes — Shimla is colonial, family-friendly, accessible. Manali is adventurous, scenic, and more exciting. For couples and adventure, Manali. For families and comfort, Shimla.

Q: Snow dekhne ke liye kab jaayein?
A: December se February mein Shimla aur Manali dono mein snow guaranteed hota hai. Lekin Rohtang February mein band hota hai. March mein bhi snow milta hai often.

Q: Kasol kaise jaate hain?
A: Delhi se overnight bus Bhuntar tak, phir local bus ya cab Kasol tak. Total ~12-14 hours. Budget travelers ke liye best route.

Q: Spiti kitne din mein cover hota hai?
A: Minimum 5-6 days for a proper circuit. Usually clubbed with Manali — Manali to Spiti to Shimla circuit of 10-12 days.

Q: Adventure activities kaunsi available hain?
A: Paragliding (Solang), river rafting (Beas), skiing (Solang/Kufri in winter), trekking (Kasol, Spiti), mountain biking, camping.
