# Destination: Kerala (Munnar + Alleppey + Kovalam + Wayanad)

## Overview
Kerala is India's most serene and scenic destination — lush tea estates, tranquil backwaters, pristine beaches, and world-class Ayurvedic wellness. Known as God's Own Country. A top choice for couples, honeymooners, and anyone seeking a peaceful, nature-immersive holiday.

## Best For
- Honeymoon and couples
- Families
- Wellness and Ayurveda seekers
- Nature and wildlife lovers
- Solo travelers

## Travel Duration
- Short: 4 Nights / 5 Days
- Standard: 6 Nights / 7 Days
- Extended: 8 Nights / 9 Days

## Getting There
- Flights to Cochin (Kochi), Trivandrum, or Calicut
- Train: Well connected from Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai
- Road: Driveable from Bangalore and Chennai

## Best Time to Visit
- Ideal: September to March
- Monsoon (July–August): Beautiful but heavy rain — Ayurveda season, good for wellness
- Avoid: April–June (peak heat)

## Package Tiers

### Budget Package — ₹12,000 to ₹18,000 per person
- Transport: Train or budget flight
- Hotel: 3-star throughout
- Meals: Daily breakfast
- Transfers: Shared or local transport
- Duration: 4N/5D (Munnar + Alleppey)
- Activities: Tea estate visit, basic houseboat (shared), Kovalam beach

### Standard Package — ₹25,000 to ₹35,000 per person
- Transport: Flights included or train
- Hotel: 4-star resorts
- Meals: Breakfast + 1 Kerala seafood dinner
- Transfers: Private cab throughout
- Duration: 6N/7D (Munnar + Alleppey + Kovalam)
- Activities: Private houseboat (1 night), tea plantation tour, Kathakali show, backwater canoe tour, beach leisure

### Premium Package — ₹50,000 to ₹70,000 per person
- Transport: Flights included
- Hotel: Luxury resorts — treehouse in Wayanad, premium houseboat, 5-star beach resort
- Meals: Breakfast + 2 special dinners
- Transfers: Private luxury cab throughout
- Duration: 7N/8D (all zones)
- Activities: Premium private houseboat (2 nights), Ayurveda spa package (2 sessions), wildlife safari in Wayanad, elephant encounter, sunset cruise, cooking class

### Luxury Package — ₹90,000 and above per person
- Transport: Flights included
- Hotel: CGH Earth, Kumarakom Lake Resort, Taj Green Cove class
- Meals: All inclusive
- Transfers: Private throughout
- Duration: 8N/9D or custom
- Activities: Full Ayurveda program, private houseboat, bespoke wildlife experiences, private cooking class, exclusive backwater exploration

## Zone Highlights

### Munnar — Tea Hills
- Tea estates stretching across rolling hills
- Eravikulam National Park — Nilgiri Tahr
- Top Station — highest point, stunning valley views
- Mattupetty Dam and Echo Point
- Blossom Park and tea museum

### Alleppey (Alappuzha) — Backwaters
- Houseboat stay on Kerala backwaters — most iconic Kerala experience
- Canoe rides through narrow village canals
- Vembanad Lake
- Kuttanad — below sea level farming region
- Alleppey Beach

### Kovalam — Beach
- Lighthouse Beach — most popular
- Hawa Beach and Samudra Beach
- Ayurveda centers and beach shacks
- Vizhinjam fishing village nearby

### Wayanad — Forests and Wildlife
- Chembra Peak trek
- Edakkal Caves — prehistoric carvings
- Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary
- Pookode Lake
- Coffee and spice plantations

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Houseboat mein ek raat rehna worth it hai?
A: Haan, absolutely — Kerala ka houseboat experience ek dum unique hai. Backwaters pe floating, fresh seafood, sunsets — unforgettable. Standard aur upar ke packages mein included hai.

Q: Honeymoon ke liye Kerala best hai kya?
A: India mein honeymoon ke liye Kerala top choice hai — romantic resorts, private houseboats, candlelit beach dinners, aur Ayurveda spa. Perfect combination.

Q: Monsoon mein jaana chahiye?
A: Agar Ayurveda ya green landscapes ke liye ja rahe ho toh July–August excellent hai. Tourism kam hota hai, rates bhi kam hote hain. Lekin outdoor activities limited ho jaati hain.

Q: Wildlife safari kahan hoga?
A: Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary ya Periyar Tiger Reserve (Thekkady) — dono premium packages mein cover hote hain.

Q: Vegetarian options milenge Kerala mein?
A: Bilkul — Kerala ka traditional saadha (vegetarian thali), avial, sambhar, idli dosa sab amazing hai. Seafood bhi excellent hai.
