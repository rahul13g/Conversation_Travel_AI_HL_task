# Destination: Japan (Tokyo + Kyoto + Osaka)

## Overview
Japan is a premium destination offering a unique blend of ultra-modern cities, ancient temples, iconic Mount Fuji, and world-renowned cuisine. Increasingly popular among Indian travelers since visa restrictions eased. Known for its safety, cleanliness, and extraordinary cultural experiences.

## Best For
- Couples and honeymoon
- Friend groups
- Families
- Solo travelers
- Culture and food enthusiasts
- Premium and luxury seekers

## Travel Duration
- Standard: 7 Nights / 8 Days
- Extended: 9 Nights / 10 Days

## Visa
- Tourist Visa required for Indians
- Processing time: 5–7 working days
- Apply through Japanese consulate or authorized agent (we handle this)
- Cost: ~₹1,500–2,000 (included in our packages)

## Best Time to Visit
- Cherry Blossom (Sakura): Late March – Early April (most popular, book early)
- Autumn Foliage: Mid-October – Mid-November (stunning)
- Summer: June–August (hot and humid, but festivals)
- Winter: December–February (snow, fewer crowds)

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Japan bohot expensive hai, kya budget mein possible hai?
A: Haan, ₹90,000–1,10,000 per person mein Japan possible hai. Flight cost thoda zyada hota hai but overall experience unmatched hai.

Q: Cherry blossom season mein jaana chahiye?
A: Agar possible ho toh haan — March end to April start. Lekin bohot popular hai, 3–4 months pehle booking karni padegi.

Q: Ryokan kya hota hai?
A: Traditional Japanese inn jahan floor pe mattress (futon) pe sote hain, private onsen (hot spring bath) hota hai, aur kaiseki dinner milta hai. Ek unique cultural experience hai.

Q: JR Pass kya hai?
A: Japan Rail Pass — ek ticket jisse aap unlimited bullet trains (Shinkansen) aur local trains use kar sakte ho. Standard aur upar ke packages mein included hai.

Q: Vegetarian khana milega?
A: Haan, hum vegetarian-friendly restaurants aur options pre-arrange karte hain. Kyoto aur Tokyo mein kaafi veg options hain.
